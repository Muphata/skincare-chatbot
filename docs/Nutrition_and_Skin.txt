Introduction:
The food you eat directly impacts skin hydration, glow, and resilience.

Key Nutrients:
- Vitamin C (citrus, strawberries) boosts collagen.
- Vitamin E (nuts, seeds) protects from oxidative stress.
- Omega-3 fatty acids (fish, flaxseed) reduce inflammation.
- Zinc supports healing and reduces acne.

Tips:
- Drink 8 glasses of water daily.
- Limit refined sugar and dairy if acne-prone.
- Choose colorful fruits and vegetables for antioxidants.
