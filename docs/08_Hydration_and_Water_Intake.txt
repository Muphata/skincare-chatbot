Proper hydration helps maintain skin elasticity, reduces dullness, and supports overall skin health. This guide explains how water and diet affect the skin.
1. Drink at least 2 liters of water daily, adjusting for activity and climate.
2. Include water-rich foods like cucumber, watermelon, and oranges.
3. Use hydrating skincare products with hyaluronic acid and glycerin.
4. Limit dehydrating beverages such as alcohol and excess caffeine.
Carry a reusable water bottle to track intake. Herbal teas can contribute to hydration. monitor urine color (light yellow = good hydration).
Overhydration is possible; avoid extreme water intake. Chronic dehydration may require medical evaluation.
