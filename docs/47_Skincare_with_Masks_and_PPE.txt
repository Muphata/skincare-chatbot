Skincare with Masks and PPE – Mini-Guide


Wearing masks or PPE for long hours can cause irritation, acne (“maskne”), and redness.
Cleanse with mild foaming cleanser after wearing PPE. Apply lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizers. Use barrier creams (zinc oxide) on friction points. protect with sunscreen when outdoors.

Choose breathable cotton masks when possible. Wash reusable masks frequently.
- Take short mask breaks in safe environments. Avoid heavy makeup under masks.
- Persistent irritation may need medical creams.
