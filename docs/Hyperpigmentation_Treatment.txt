Hyperpigmentation Treatment – Mini-Guide

Introduction:
Dark spots appear from sun damage, acne scars, or hormones.

Ingredients:
- Vitamin C brightens skin tone.
- Niacinamide reduces dark patches.
- Azelaic acid fades pigmentation safely.
- Sunscreen prevents worsening.

Tips:
- Be patient: results take 8–12 weeks.
- Avoid hydroquinone without medical guidance.
