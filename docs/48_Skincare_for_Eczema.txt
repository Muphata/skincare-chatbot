Skincare for Eczema eczema is a chronic inflammatory condition causing dry, itchy, red patches. Skincare helps manage flare-ups. Use fragrance-free, gentle cleansers. Apply thick moisturizers (ointments or creams) after bathing. Use medicated creams (steroids or calcineurin inhibitors) if prescribed. Protect skin with SPF. Apply moisturizer within 3 minutes after showering (“soak and seal”).  Wear cotton clothing to reduce irritation. Identify and avoid triggers (detergents, stress). 

- Avoid hot showers and harsh soaps.
- Persistent flare-ups need medical attention.
