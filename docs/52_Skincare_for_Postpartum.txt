Skincare for Postpartum after childbirth, skin may be sensitive, acne-prone, or dry. A balanced routine helps recovery.

Cleanse gently twice daily. Apply lightweight moisturizers with ceramides.
 Use brightening serums (vitamin C) for pigmentation.  Protect with SPF daily.

Simplify routine during busy schedules. Sleep and hydration directly affect skin health. 
Treat stretch marks gently with moisturizing oils.

Avoid strong actives if breastfeeding without doctor approval. Persistent skin issues may require professional care.
