Skincare for Sensitive Eye
The skin around eyes is thin and prone to irritation. Extra care is needed for this area.

Remove makeup with micellar water or gentle balms. Apply fragrance-free eye creams with peptides.

Use mineral sunscreen safe for eye area. Hydrate with cooling gels.

store eye creams in the fridge for puffiness. Tap gently with ring finger when applying.

Use sunglasses for extra protection.

Avoid essential oils or strong acids near eyes. Persistent irritation may need medical advice.
