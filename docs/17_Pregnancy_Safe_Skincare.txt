Pregnancy : Pregnancy changes skin due to hormones. Some ingredients must be avoided for safety.

Cleanse with gentle, non-medicated cleansers. Use pregnancy-safe actives like niacinamide or azelaic acid.
Moisturize with shea butter or ceramides. Protect with mineral sunscreen (zinc oxide).


Treat stretch marks with oils or creams containing vitamin E and cocoa butter.
 Keep routines simple and hydrating.Manage acne with azelaic acid instead of retinoids.


- Avoid retinoids, hydroquinone, and salicylic acid in high concentrations. Always consult your OB/GYN before new treatments.
