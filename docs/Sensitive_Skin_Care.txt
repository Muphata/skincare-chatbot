Sensitive Skin Care – Mini-Guide

Introduction:
Sensitive skin easily reacts with redness, burning, or itching.

Do’s:
- Use fragrance-free, alcohol-free cleansers.
- Patch test new products.
- Apply soothing ingredients (aloe vera, panthenol).

Don’ts:
- Avoid scrubs and harsh exfoliants.
- Limit active acids unless supervised.
