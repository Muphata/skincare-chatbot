Topic: Exfoliation (AHA, BHA, PHA)

Exfoliants remove dead skin cells.

Types:
- AHA: glycolic, lactic – brighten, hydrate.
- BHA: salicylic acid – unclog pores, acne.
- PHA: gentle, for sensitive skin.

Frequency:
- 1–3 times/week.
- Start slowly, avoid mixing with retinoids.

Warnings:
- Always wear sunscreen.
- Over-exfoliation causes irritation.
