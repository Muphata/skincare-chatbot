Sun Protection – Mini-Guide

Introduction:
UV radiation accelerates aging, causes hyperpigmentation, and raises skin cancer risk.

Steps:
1. Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ every morning.
2. Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours outdoors.
3. Wear wide-brim hats and sunglasses.
4. Seek shade between 10 am – 4 pm.

Warnings:
- Even on cloudy days, UV penetrates the skin.
- Avoid tanning beds.
