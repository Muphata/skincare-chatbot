Scars Treatment 
Acne scars are long-term reminders of breakouts. Treatments depend on type (pigmentation, rolling scars, ice-pick).

Use sunscreen daily to prevent scars from darkening. apply niacinamide and vitamin C for pigmentation. Consider chemical peels, microneedling, or lasers for deeper scars. Keep skin moisturized to support healing.
