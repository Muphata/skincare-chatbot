Skincare for Kids
Children’s skin is more sensitive and delicate. Gentle routines are best. Bathe with mild, fragrance-free cleansers. Apply fragrance-free moisturizers daily.

- Avoid adult products on kids’ skin and Persistent rashes may need pediatric evaluation.

Use mineral sunscreen when outdoors. Keep nails trimmed to reduce scratching.

Dress children in cotton clothes to reduce irritation. Apply ointments on dry patches immediately after bathing. use lip balms with SPF for outdoor play.
